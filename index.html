About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8‑week hands‑on program that teaches professionals how to harness no‑code AI tools to automate data analysis, content creation, knowledge management, and routine tasks.

  • Automate time‑consuming document, spreadsheet, and data tasks
  • Organize knowledge and conduct literature reviews efficiently
  • Develop custom chatbots to support projects and teams
  • Create professional visuals and presentations with AI
  • Apply learning in a capstone project with real‑time support through workshops and forums
